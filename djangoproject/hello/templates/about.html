About SmartMate

SmartMate is an innovative stress detection and prediction system that uses Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) to analyze various physiological parameters and determine stress levels. Our model takes into account multiple factors including sleep patterns, vital signs, and physical movements to provide accurate stress assessments.

The system helps users track their mental health by monitoring key indicators and providing timely insights about their stress levels. This enables users to take proactive steps in managing their mental well-being.
